About

I'm a writer and editor, and looking for work in the publishing industry. As a student at University of Southern California, I studied both Creative Writing and International Relations. I worked on numerous student publications as a writer and editor, an experience that suplemented my coursework as a creative writer.

As a student at New York University's Summer Publishing Institute, I learned from industry experts, authors, editors, and more about the publishing world. I also developed my own skills, both technical and creative. Between team projects, panels, and presentations, I still made time to enjoy NYC.

While living and studying in Spain, I developed my lanuguage skills. Even more than honing my Spanish, I learned to be adaptable, curious, organized, and adventurous.

May of 2023, I graduated from USC Phi Beta Kappa. I'm looking forward to tackling the daunting world of publishing, wherever it may take me.
